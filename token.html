FAQs

All your questions about the Easy CryptoToken answered.

What are the benefits of holding Easy CryptoTokens?

The Easy CryptoToken is our native cryptocurrency which brings token holders numerous benefits on our platform.

Holders receive up to 12% interest per annum on the Easy CryptoTokens held in both the Savings and Credit Line wallets of their Easy Cryptoaccounts.

Also, holding Easy CryptoTokens automatically makes you a part of Easy crpto Loyalty Program which gives you:

  • Up to 50% higher yields with our Earn on Crypto suite.
  • Up to 0.5% back in crypto rewards on purchases or swaps via the Easy CryptoExchange.
  • Borrowing rates starting from just 0% APR.
  • Up to 5 free crypto withdrawals.

And last but not least, holding Easy CryptoTokens gives you the opportunity to participate in any future Governance Votes thus designing the future of Easy Crypto(1 Easy Crypto= 1 Vote).

Read more about our Loyalty Program and the perks of our native token here .

Where can I buy Easy CryptoTokens?

You can acquire Easy CryptoTokens through the Easy CryptoExchange inside our platform (recommended) or through a third-party exchange.

Buying Easy CryptoTokens on the Easy CryptoExchange

This is the easiest and quickest way to buy Easy CryptoTokens without leaving our platform. Here are the steps:

  1. From the Dashboard, tap on the “Add Funds” option and select the “Credit/Debit Card”.
  2. Pick Easy Cryptoas the asset you want to purchase and enter how much you would like to spend.
  3. Enter your credit or debit card details. 
  4. Confirm your purchase and you’re done!

Note: You can now also top up USDx, GBPx or EURx and swap them directly for Easy Cryptowith our NEXO/fiat pairs.

Will Easy CryptoTokens used as collateral also earn interest?

Yes, all Easy CryptoTokens used as collateral will also earn interest. It doesn’t matter if your tokens are in your Credit Line or Savings Wallet as long as they are in your Easy Cryptoaccount.
