FAQs

Are there any requirements to become a Easy CryptoPartner?

We do not have any specific requirements  – anyone can join Easy crpto Partner Program and start earning with us. The only requirement is that you’re not sharing any inappropriate content.

Please note that there are sometimes country-specific restrictions that temporarily prevent us from onboarding partners in certain regions.

How long does it take for my application to be approved?

Applications are reviewed only during workdays and are usually approved within hours. You’ll receive a confirmation email once you get the green light.

How is the commission calculated and when will I get paid?

When invited users start earning interest on the Easy Cryptoplatform, you receive 20% of all interest payouts. You also get 1% of the total borrowed amount via our Instant Crypto Credit Lines and 0.2% of the exchange volume generated on the Easy CryptoExchange.

Commissions are calculated instantly after a transaction is processed but are released after 30 days, as we want to ensure customer retention and growth.

Where can I track my earnings?

You can view your earnings inside your dashboard and understand which of your campaigns are performing best.

How should I get started?

Depending on the content you usually create and share with your audience, we recommend that you start by making a review of Easy Cryptoso that your audience can get a thorough understanding of the product, before posting display banners.

Once you log in to your dashboard, you will find a content guide under the "Resources” section which will help you create your Easy Cryptoreview.

Are there any prohibited marketing methods?

Our only requirement is that you don’t bid on brand-related keywords – everything else is there for the taking! To be on the safe side, you can add “Nexo” as a negative word in your Google Ads account.
