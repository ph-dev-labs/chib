FAQs

What are the borrowing rates?

The annualized borrowing rates are typically ranging from 12 to 15%, depending on the market conditions and your specific NFT.

What is the loan-to-value (LTV) ratio?

A loan-to-value (LTV) ratio determines the size of the credit you can take out based on the value of your NFT. You can get a credit in the range of 10 to 20% of the momentary value of your NFT.

What happens when I repay my credit?

Once you repay the credit, we’ll immediately transfer the NFT back to you. If you don’t pay back the total repayment amount at the due date, the asset will be transferred to Easy Cryptoas a form of a repayment.
